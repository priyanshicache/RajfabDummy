About the Department
Factory & Boiler
Inspection Department

The Government of Rajasthan established the Factories and Boilers Inspection Department to inspect factories and boilers, enforcing the Factories Act, 1948 and Boilers Act, 1923, ensuring the safety, health and welfare of workers across the state.

As part of the Ease of Doing Business initiative, the department has achieved 100% compliance of the Business Reform Action Plan (BRAP-2019), making compliance simpler and more transparent for industries.

Learn more
Safety First

Enforcing Factories Act 1948 & Boilers Act 1923 to ensure workplace safety and worker welfare across Rajasthan.

Industrial Hygiene Lab

Dedicated laboratory at Jaipur for air pollutant sampling, analysis and corrective measures for occupational health.

Safety Museum & Training

SMTC at Jaipur conducts periodic training sessions for workers, supervisors and managers by safety experts.

Occupational Health

Certified surgeons provide on-site medical check-ups and occupational health assessments for factory workers.
